# Expensify trust-section reference

The current Expensify homepage places the trust section immediately after the hero. It is a **single uninterrupted dark-green band**, not a collection of cards.

## Exact composition

- A centered, one-line serif heading reads: “Join 15 million+ members who trust Expensify.”
- The heading is modest in size and white, with generous space above and below.
- Beneath it is one horizontal row of white, monochrome customer logos.
- The logo row behaves like a continuous marquee: the same sequence repeats, and logos are evenly spaced with substantial gaps.
- There are no descriptions, icons in boxes, borders, shadows, pills, metrics, or secondary copy.
- The section is visually shallow—approximately 140–155px tall on desktop—and transitions directly into the next cream-colored section.
